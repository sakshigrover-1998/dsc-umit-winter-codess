About

Winter Codess : A DSC-UMIT chapter to help bright minds reach new heights.

Well-balanced college life is a dream for many but is rarely achieved. Handling curriculum, councils, co-curricular & learning new things that you are interested in while maintaining a social life can be hectic. We are here to help you reach a step closer to learning new things. Learning something new can be a little frustrating; even the ocean of knowledge on the internet can prove to be a bane rather than a boon. Here is where Winter Codess comes in. In this program, you will be mentored and guided through an exciting journey of new oppurtunities that'll help you make a minimum viable product.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • When will the program start?

    We intend to start the program by 15th December and end by January 2021. *(Subject to change for exam schedule).

  • Who will be the mentors?

    The mentors are industry people, who have experience working and developing projects. A co-mentor will be available from the DSC Team, to co-ordinate and guide along.

  • What will be the mode of guiding?

    The mode will be through the virtual platform. It is with the consensus of the group to decide on suitable days and timings for the meetings and reviews.

  • Does the project needs to be started from scratch?

    Basic knowledge about the domain is required. The project will start from scratch with each team-member contributing equally.

  • Is this program by Google?

    No. This is a program conducted by Developer Students Club - UMIT .

  • Will certificates be provided?

    Yes. Certificates will be provided on sucessfull completeion of the program.

  • Can an individual bring their ideas?

    Yes. You can contribute to an idea and let your mentor know about it

  • Can non-umitians participate in Winter codess?

    Yes. Winter Codess is open for all the aspirants with all levels of experience and expertise.

  • How will we be assigned in groups?

    You will be assigned in groups according to the topic selected. The groups will consist 3-4 people.
